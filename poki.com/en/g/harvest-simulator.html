Harvest Simulator

Camu4.554,413 votes

Harvest Simulator invites you to experience the life of a modern-day farmer. Take the wheel of your trusty tractor to harvest a wide variety of crops across vibrant fields. Manage your resources, upgrade your equipment, and overcome real farming challenges in this engaging, realistic simulation. Are you ready to claim this bountiful harvest?

How do I play Harvest Simulator?

Use your mouse to interact with buttons!

  • Forward: W or the up arrow key
  • Reverse: S or the down arrow key
  • Turn: Use the A and D or left and right arrow keys to turn
  • Camera Adjustment: E
